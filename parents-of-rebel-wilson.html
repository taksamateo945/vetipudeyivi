parents of rebel wilson: Who are Rebel wilsons parents ?

Publish date: 2024-07-12

After her studies at ‘Australian Theatre for Young People,’ Rebel Wilson got a scholarship to New York from Nicole Kidman.

She began her TV career on ‘Pizza’ after gaining attention from stage work.

Her debut film, ‘Fat Pizza,’ came from the TV series of the same name.

She took on various roles in ‘The Wedge’ (2006-2007) and appeared in several TV shows.

In 2012, she starred in films like ‘Bachelorette’ and ‘Pitch Perfect’ and voiced Raz in ‘Ice Age: Continental Drift.’

In 2013, she joined ‘Super Fun Night’ and hosted the ‘MTV Movie Awards.’

She continued with ‘Pitch Perfect’ sequels, ‘Grimsby,’ ‘Absolutely Fabulous: The Movie,’ and ‘Little Mermaid.’

2019, she starred in ‘Isn’t It Romantic’ and ‘The Hustle.’

In 2020, she hosted ‘Pooch Perfect’ and ‘LOL: Last One Laughing,’ and appeared on ‘Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?’

Year 2022, she featured in ‘Senior Year’ and ‘The Almond and the Seahorse,’ both with mixed reviews.
