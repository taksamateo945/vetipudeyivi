2023 BMW M5 All Price, Top Speed, 0-60 mph &amp; Specifications

Publish date: 2024-02-04

The BMW M5 is the mightiest amongst all BMW M-badged cars in the company’s lineup. The 2023 BMW M5 aka the grand daddy of all 5-Series cars with its magnanimous power and absolutely freaking performance with exquisite interior and its serene design. The new M5 is nothing but an improved specced version of the that came out two years before that. Under the hood lies the 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 that produces over 600 hp of thrill. Driving through its all-wheel drive system controlled by an engaging 8-speed automatic transmission. The outburst of power can be increased to 17 more ponies with incredible exhaust sounds with the optional Competition package. It results in an outrageous amount of performance and driving confidence with crazy acceleration in just 2.8 seconds.

What’s New In 2023 BMW M5?

For 2023, the BMW M5 comes with some minor and subtle changes. The M5 Competition is not offered as an individual model but rather offered as an upgrade with optional equipment packages. The M5 also receives some new color options such as San Remo Green and Skyscraper Grey metallic options for this year. While the colors like Hatch Grey, SNapper Rocks Blue, Motengi Red, Donington Grey options are all discontinued. Inside, the M5 offers new Smoke White/Black and Tartufo Ful Merino leather upholstery as the material options.

2023 BMW M5 Engine Specifications & Performance

The obnoxious performance is delivered by the M5’s insanely quick and outrageous engine under its hood. The M5 offers a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces over 600 hp and 553 lb-ft of torque. With the Competition package, the power increases to 617 hp resulting in a rapid acceleration from 0-60 mph in just 2.8 seconds. The car can reach a top speed of a crazy 190 mph with its powerful V8 engine. It comes paired with a fuild 8-speed automatic gearbox and standard all-wheel drive.

The performance as you would expect from a flagship 5-Series is just top notch with superior throttle response and power delivery. The BMW M5 excels with its flawless driving capabilities and insane ride quality combined with a high-tech and luxurious interior. You can drive the 2023 M5 for both daily driving and cruising thanks to its versatility in smooth ride throughout. It is a compelling partner no matter what type of driver you are, it will always result in incredibly fast and spirited driving.

Fuel Economy of 2023 BMW M5

The 2023 M5 being the mightiest of all 5-Series cars should not be regarded as a fuel-efficient car. Its standard all-wheel drive along with the massively powerful twin-turbo V8 is a thirsty engine. The 2023 M5 delivers a fuel economy that is rated 15 mpg in the city and 21 mpg on the highway by the EPA. As BM’s have a track record of understating their fuel efficiency, the M5 will deliver more than the claimed mileage.

2023 BMW M5 Dimensions, Space & Airbags

Despite its long hooded design and bulky body, the BMW M5 is a spirited animal with its ferociousness and a real grudge for power. It provides excellent cornering confidence and gripper handling regardless of the surface. The upside of its long chassis is the car’s insanely well-designed interior fitted with rich materials and tech to provide almost comfort.

Pricing

The 2023 BMW M5 with its outrageous burst of power and superior performance still feels like a no-brainer compared to the level of comfort the BMW M5 offers. This good design combination of power and beauty doesn’t come cheap as the car starts with a six-figure price tag. Plus the added features from the level of sportiness and luxury it offers with its customization options. The 2023 M5 standard with its 600 hp V8 starts at around $110,000. If you throw in the Competition package with tuned suspension and chassis and other dynamics, the price could reach well over $120,000.
