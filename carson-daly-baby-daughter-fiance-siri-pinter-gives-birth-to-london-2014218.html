Carson Daly Baby Daughter: Fiancee Siri Pinter Gives Birth to London

Publish date: 2024-06-13

It's a Daly baby! Carson Daly became a father for the third time on Wednesday, Aug. 20, when his fiancée Siri Pinter gave birth to a baby girl. 

The couple welcomed daughter London Rose Daly, after announcing in March that they were expecting their third child together. Daly and Pinter are already the parents to son Jackson James, 5, and daughter Etta Jones, 23 months. 

Just last week, Pinter joked about her impending birth, tweeting, "It's pretty amusing how frightened strangers are of me right now. Especially when I scream QUICK CATCH THE BABY!! #10monthspregnant." London was born at 8 lbs. 11 oz., the Today show reports, and measured 22 inches long. 

The new addition to the Daly family already has her first friend lined up, as Daly's Today show colleague Savannah Guthrie welcomed her new baby Vale last week. On Thursday, Aug. 14, Pinter tweeted, "Congrats @SavannahGuthrie on your baby girl!! Now I'm even more anxious for little Vale's BFF to be born :)"

After Vale's new bestie arrived, London, Daly, and Pinter were quickly congratulated by their friends and coworkers. Today show EP Don Nash tweeted, "Can you say awwwww?" while anchor Natalie Morales chimed in, "Congrats @CarsonDaly and gorgeous wife Siri on another beautiful baby, London Rose. Hope Jack and Etta are loving their sis."

"Siri is a warrior and I'm so amazed by her poise, grace and strength during this miraculous process," the Voice host, 41, told Today. "Watching Jack and Etta meet their new sister was like watching a scene in a movie, directed by God."

Daly proposed to his food blogger love in October 2013 after dating for six years.
