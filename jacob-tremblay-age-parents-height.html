Know About Jacob Tremblay; Age, Parents, Height, Room, Movies, Insta

Publish date: 2024-04-26

Just 12-years-old in age, Jacob Tremblay has already made a name for himself in the acting industry. The young actor is famous for his breakthrough performance of Jack Newsome in the 2015 drama flick, Room.

Jacob Tremblay is actively involved in the showbiz since 2013. In the process, he has already made his presence in around two dozen movies and TV shows.

Jacob Tremblay Short Bio: Age, Parents, Sister, Height

The 12-years-of-age actor was born as Jacob Tremblay on 5 October 2006 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. He is the son of his parents, Jason Tremblay and Christina Candia Tremblay. Jacob’s dad, Jason, is a police detective while his mother is a homemaker. His older sister, Emma Tremblay and younger sister, Erica Tremblay are also involved in acting business.

Jacob Tremblay with his parents at the Great Wall of China on 18 January 2018 (Picture: Jacob’s Instagram)

About 4 feet and 8 inches (1.42 m) tall in height, Jacob completed his schooling from Gordon Greenwood Elementary School, British Columbia, Canada. As of now, he resides in Langley.

Career In TV And Movies

The young and talented actor commenced his acting career appearing in two series named Motive and Mr. Young in 2013. The same year, he ringed his big-screen debut as Blue Winslow in the 3D animated comedy film, The Smurfs 2. Eventually, Jacob landed a big career break with the Oscar-nominated drama flick, Room, in 2015.

Jacob Tremblay as Jack Newsome on Room (Picture: IMDB)

With his praise-worthy performance on Room, Jacob bagged the role in several other movies like Before I Wake (2016), Burn Tour Maps (2016), The Book of Henry (2017), The Predator (2018), and Good Boys (2019). While speaking of his TV career, he amasses popularity with The Last Man on Earth (2016), American Dad! (2017), Pete the Cat (2017-present), and The Twilight Zone (2019).

Jacob is set to voice Robin in the upcoming DC Universe adult animated series, Harley Quinn. 

What About Jacob’s Relationship Life?

To be fair, the actor is a few years away from worrying about dating and relationship stuff. To the recent, he is enjoying a promising entertainment career and thinking of taking it to the next level.

  • His BB-8 (Star Wars droid character) appearance at the 88th Academy Awards ceremony was considered the highlight of the evening.
  • He is good friends with actress Brie Larson.
