Twinnie Shares 'I Love You Now Change' Music Video

Publish date: 2024-03-03

British country crossover sensation Twinnie has today (Thursday, March 26th) shared the official music video for her brand new single ‘I Love You Now Change’.

The track is the latest hit to be lifted off her forthcoming and much anticipated debut album ‘Hollywood Gypsy’ – the record will be available to stream, download, and purchase on CD from Friday, April 17th and fans are able to pre-order now ahead of its release.

Meanwhile, ‘I Love You Now Change’ is an anthemic banger that incorporates classic country lyrics with a meaningful pop sensibility delivering fresh hooks and inspirational message of self worth, showcasing yet another side of the LP’s sonic vibe.

And the official video brings the songs message to life perfectly with Twinnie offering her fans a fantastic, cinematic piece of film.

Shot in London, directed by Chris Turner, and starring Gustav Wood, the clip’s treatment focuses on a relationship, showing loved up scenes and progressing into how the dynamic breaks down leading to divorce.

It’s a powerful piece of filmography that highlights the lyrical backdrop of how you have to have a power within to be truly who you are and never change that for anyone.

This piece of video art truly shines a light on the incredible artistry Twinnie possesses and showcases her immense acting skills alongside her power vocals.
