Zoe Zobrist shares new coming-of-age single 'Who You Gonna Be'

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Zoe Zobrist has shared her new single ‘Who You Gonna Be’ which comes with a retro-styled visual.

The track is the first to be released off her upcoming project and groups together seductive vocals, droplets of deliberate self-reflection, and a lifting pop chorus as the crowning stroke.

Speaking about the track, she said: “Every day, every moment, we have the opportunity to trust ourselves, to take risks and push past uncertainty. To reach down into the depths of our being and make the decision to live the best life we can, while we can. ‘Who You Gonna Be’ is about the coming-of-age adventure, no matter how old you are.“

‘Who You Gonna Be’ is out now via Chirp Entertainment. Buy/stream the track here and check out the chic visual below:

The 24-year-old alt-rock singer-songwriter hails from Dallas, Texas and has seen a plethora of success on the live circuit with hundreds of shows under her belt including appearances at SXSW, Los Angeles’ The Viper Room, The Troubadour, and Dallas Fort Worth’s Six Flags Amphitheatre.

The artist has so far released two EPs and is gearing up for the release of her latest project later in the year.
