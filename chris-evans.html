Chris Evans Bio, Age &amp; Height

Publish date: 2024-07-23

As a teenager, Chris wanted to be a painter. His mother is an art director and Chris gets his love for art from her.

Chris has given his voice to the video games – Fantastic Four (2005), and Captain America: Super Soldier (2011).

He is a supporter of same-sex marriage and rights.

Has signed on to do three Fantastic Four films.

He participated in an advertising campaign for perfume Gucci Guilty and Guilty pour Homme with actress Evan Rachel Wood.

Chris Evans height is 6 Feet 0 Inches (182.9 centimeters tall).

Chris has Italian and Irish ancestry.
