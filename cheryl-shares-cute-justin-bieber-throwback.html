Cheryl shares cute Justin Bieber throwback

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Cheryl Fernandez-Versini has shared a cute throwback photo of her with pop mega-star Justin Bieber, on her official Instagram page.

The ‘I Don’t Care’ hitmaker shared the adorable snap earlier today to celebrate throwback Thursday.

The picture of the smiling popstars was taken backstage at the Radio 1’s Big Weekend, in Bangor North Wales, way back in 2010 when Justin Bieber was only 16 years old and just starting to make a name for himself here in the UK.

The X Factor judge simply captioned the throwback post: #tbt baby biebs (sic).

Over the years Bieber has spoken about his admiration of Cheryl and a few years back the ‘What Do You Mean?’ singer spoke to New! magazine about his love for the X Factor judge saying: “Every time I’ve met her she has been so sweet to me. She really does have the whole package. I like her as an artist as well, she is very talented”. 

Justin also added: “I don’t think there is a guy that doesn’t. If I was older I would have asked her out for dinner when I was single. She’s really concentrating on her music and movies at the moment but, when she does decide to settle down, he’s going to be a lucky guy.”

Since it is throwback Thursday why don’t you enjoy this  2010 X Factor performance from Justin Bieber, where her performed a mash up of his hit singles ‘Somebody To Love’ and ‘Baby’.
