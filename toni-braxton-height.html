Toni Braxton Awards and Recognition

Toni Braxton’s talent and success have earned her numerous awards and recognition in the music industry. From prestigious accolades to critical acclaim, her contributions are widely celebrated.

Grammy Awards

Braxton has won a total of 7 Grammy Awards throughout her career. Her debut self-titled album, “Toni Braxton,” won Best New Artist in 1994. She also received Grammy Awards for her hit single “Un-Break My Heart” in the categories of Record of the Year, Best Female Pop Vocal Performance, and Best Female R&B Vocal Performance. Braxton’s albums “Secrets” and “Love, Marriage & Divorce” also earned her Grammy recognition.

Billboard Music Awards

Braxton has been honored with multiple Billboard Music Awards. Her song “Un-Break My Heart” won the prestigious Hot 100 Airplay and Hot Adult Contemporary Track of the Year in 1997. She has also received awards for Top R&B Artist, Top Female R&B Artist, and Top R&B Album.

American Music Awards

Throughout her career, Braxton has received several American Music Awards. She won Favorite Female Soul/R&B Artist in 1994 and 1997. Her album “Secrets” also earned her the award for Favorite Soul/R&B Album in 1997.
