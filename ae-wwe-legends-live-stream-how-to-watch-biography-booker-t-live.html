A&amp;E WWE Legends Live Stream: How To Watch Biography: Booker T Live

Publish date: 2024-01-20

If you’ve been enjoying A&E’s WWE Legends series, you’ll be happy to know that the next episode centers on the six time (six time, six time, etc) world champion Booker T.

Directed by Emmy-winner George Roy, Biography: Booker T highlights the life and career of one of the most charismatic performers in professional wrestling. From his successful tag team runs with his brother Stevie Ray and Goldust to his memorable feuds with The Rock, “Stone Cold” Steve Austin, and Chris Benoit, the 2006 King of the Ring-winner and two-time WWE Hall of Famer never failed to entertain.

Four more episodes of WWE Legends remain. Next week’s installment focuses on the career of the “Heartbreak Kid” Shawn Michaels, followed by The Ultimate Warrior, Mick Foley, and Bret “Hitman” Hart.

How can you watch Biography: Booker T live on A&E? Here’s everything you need to know.

WHEN DOES THE NEXT EPISODE OF BIOGRAPHY: WWE LEGENDS PREMIERE ON A&E?

The fourth episode of Biography: WWE Legends premieres Sunday, May 9 on A&E.

WHAT TIME DOES BIOGRAPHY: BOOKER T START ON A&E?

Biography: Booker T airs from 8:00-10:02 p.m. ET on A&E. An encore presentation of the documentary airs Monday, May 10 at 12:04 a.m. ET.

HOW TO WATCH A&E’S WWE LEGENDS: BOOKER T DOCUMENTARY LIVE ONLINE:

If you have a valid cable login, you can watch Biography: Booker T live on A&E’s website or with the A&E app. The special will also be available for next-day streaming on A&E.com.

HOW TO WATCH A&E’S BIOGRAPHY: WWE LEGENDS SERIES LIVE WITHOUT CABLE:

You can also find an A&E live stream with an active subscription to Hulu + Live TV, Sling TV (Blue), fuboTV, or Philo. All of the aforementioned services offer A&E in their channel lineups. Free trials of the streaming services are available for new and eligible subscribers.

DOES HULU OFFER A&E?

You can’t stream Biography: WWE Legends with a traditional Hulu account, but you can watch the series live or on demand with an active subscription to Hulu + Live TV. Hulu’s live TV service includes an A&E live stream. A seven-day free trial is available for eligible subscribers.

WILL A&E’S BOOKER T DOCUMENTARY BE ON HULU?

Unfortunately, the series isn’t available for next-day streaming on Hulu.
