Who is Nore:

Nore is a famous Rapper. He was born on September 6, 1977 and his birthplace is Queens, NY. Nore is also well known as, New York rapper also known as Noreaga or P.A.P.I. most famous for being a member of the rap duo Capone-N-Noreaga, a.k.a. C-N-N. He released his Gold-selling self-titled debut album in 1998.

Nore is originated from United States. His 2007 album “Noreality” featured contributions from superstar rapper and produer Kanye West.
