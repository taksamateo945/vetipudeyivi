Cristiano Ronaldo Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo was born in Madeira, Portugal, on February 5, 1985, to Jose Dinis Aveiro and Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro. His father worked as a city gardener, while his mother was a chef. Ronaldo was the youngest of four children, including a son and two daughters. Ronaldo was dismissed from school when he beat his instructor with a chair. He had always been a dedicated player, and by the age of fourteen, he had chosen to focus on being a professional footballer. Ronaldo had been playing for Androrinha since he was eight years old.
