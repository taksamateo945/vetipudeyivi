David Beckham Gets Sweet New Tattoo for Daughter Harper: Pic

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Doting dad! David Beckham showed his love for his 4-year-old daughter, Harper, in one adorable new way.

The soccer star, 40, took to Instagram on Sunday, July 26, to share a photo of a new tattoo that he had inked on the bottom of his neck. Above a previous tattoo reading simply, "Harper," Beckham added in script, "Pretty Lady," with a tiny heart.

"Hey Pretty Lady…." the father of four wrote with a heart emoji. "Just adding to the collection of tattoos that mean so much to me… Thanks Mark as always looks amazing…"

Beckham celebrated his pretty lady's 4th birthday earlier this month. He shared a sweet throwback photo with Harper on July 10, writing, "Possibly my favourite picture of Harper of all time… 4 beautiful years with the most amazing little girl… Happy birthday big girl.."

The star, who has been sporting the Harper tattoo since 2011, expanded his tribute to his daughter just days after he showed off another recently inked tattoo. The British athlete honored his wife, Victoria Beckham, on the couple's 15th wedding anniversary with a permanent token of love.

On Wednesday, July 22, Beckham posted a photo of his hand, which now sports a tiny number "99" on his pinky finger. "99 was a good year for me…" he wrote, nodding to the year that he and Victoria exchanged vows and welcomed their first child, son Brooklyn.
