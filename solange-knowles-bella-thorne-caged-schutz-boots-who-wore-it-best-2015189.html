Solange Knowles, Bella Thorne Rock the Same Caged Boots: Poll

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Day and night! Solange Knowles and Bella Thorne are both fans of the Schutz “Karlyanna” caged knee-high heel, but clearly, the two ladies have very different thoughts on how to style them.

Going a more traditional route, Knowles, 29, laced up her fishnet-style boots (which retail for $395) underneath a long-sleeved, white lace dress to take in the 3.1 Phillip Lim show at New York Fashion Week on Monday, Sept. 14. White shades and natural curls completed her ensemble.
