Kim Basingers Mystery Man Revealed Shes Dating Her Longtime Hairstylist!

Publish date: 2024-05-10

The identity of Kim Basinger’s new man is no longer a mystery!

‘Closer Weekly’ can exclusively reveal that the man she was recently spotted cuddling up to while on vacation in Hawaii earlier in December is her new beau, and longtime hairstylist, Mitch Stone!

“After doing her hair for so many years, one day they thought, Hey, why don’t we go out on a date?” an insider exclusively reveals in the new issue of ‘Closer.’ “And they have been very, very happy together.”

Kim with her second husband, Alec, in 1999.

The 61-year-old Oscar-winning actress and her new man “were very affectionate and always touching each other,” an eyewitness explains of the couple during their December vacation. “They went swimming in the ocean, and they went shopping. They looked relaxed, happy and very much in love.”
