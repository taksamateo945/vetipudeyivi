Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna are getting their own TV show

Publish date: 2024-02-11

E! has revealed that Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna will be starring in their own docu-series on their channel. The couple has shot six one-hour episodes which will feature everything from their engagement to Chyna’s surprise pregnancy. The working title is ‘Rob & Chyna’.

Other than the television show, the two have given E! the right to air a special about the birth of their first child together. Executive Vice President of Original Programming & Development at E!, Jeff Olde, said, “Very few love stories have created as much pop culture buzz as Rob and Chyna’s, and we are thrilled to see Rob in such a happy place. We are excited to share the next chapter in their relationship.”

‘Rob & Chyna’ will be produced by Ryan Seacrest Productions and Bunim/Murray Productions just like ‘Keeping Up with the Kardashians’. Both Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna will executive produce the show. The docu-series is set to air later this year.

Although there has been controversy surrounding this relationship, family insiders have revealed that the couple is as happy as they can be, and that the Kardashians have accepted it. They said, “The family is happy to see how bright Rob is and that’s what they care about. Blac and Rob are so excited to have their baby! Rob is making sure Chyna takes it easy and does not over-do it.”
