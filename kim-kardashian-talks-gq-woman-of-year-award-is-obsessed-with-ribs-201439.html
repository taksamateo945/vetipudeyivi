Kim Kardashian Talks GQ Woman of Year Award, Is &quot;Obsessed With Ribs&quot;

Publish date: 2024-01-31

Three cheers for the Woman of The Year! Kim Kardashian was awarded the title by GQ UK on Tuesday, Sept. 2, an award she said during an interview with BBC's Radio 1 on Wednesday, Sept. 3, she thinks is "sexy."

"I like to think of it as a sexy award and fun … I think it was just such a cool honor," the reality star said in an interview with Nick Grimshaw. Kanye West's wife added that she was only joking about not being properly addressed as Kim Kardashian West.

"They announced me as Kim Kardashian…I hate that people online say mean things because when I got up there I was like, 'It’s Kim Kardashian West,' just [joking]," she laughed. "But on the Internet it was like, ‘She’s annoyed they called her Kim Kardashian.' [I'm like,] 'No I’m not!'"

When asked where she'll keep the coveted award, North West's mom said West isn't big on displaying their awards around their home.

"Kanye’s not big on putting Grammys up and stuff like that. [His Grammys] were literally in the laundry room in a random sock drawer…so I’ve gathered them all and put them in a box in my room until we move, [but] maybe I can talk him into putting them in one of our offices or something. He’s just not into awards all over the house," she explained. "He’s not a show-off person." 

Kardashian, on the other hand, said she'll place hers where she can see it! "I will put mine in the middle of my glam room. It’s so cool," she gushed.

After gushing that she loves being a wife, the 33-year-old went on to dish about her favorite food while in London. "Every time I come to London, I have an obsession with ribs. Like, last night we were rushing to get to Hakkasan after the awards and we just sat and ate so many ribs … It’s my favorite thing to do when I’m in London. And it’s low carb. I’m obsessed with ribs."
