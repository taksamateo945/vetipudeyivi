Peyton List, Ajiona Alexus, Brent Rivera, &amp; More Spill Spooky Secrets from Filming Light As A Fea

Publish date: 2024-03-19

With Halloween right around the corner, there are SO many things we need to do to officially get in that holiday spirit. One of them? Binging EVERY horror show and movie that’s out on the market!

Luckily for us, our friends over at Hulu and AwesomessTV are giving us something to add to our list of shows and movies to watch, and it’s based off the innocent game we all know and love — Light as a Feather, Stiff as a Board.

Appropriately titled Light As A Feather, the series follows five teen girls who play an innocent game of “Light as a Feather, Stiff as a Board” that goes wrong when they start dying off in the exact way that was predicted. The survivors are forced to figure out why they’re being targeted – and whether the evil force hunting them down is one of their own.

On Thursday, September 27th, the cast of the horror series came together to celebrate the world premiere at the AwesomenessTV headquarters in Los Angeles! Stars like Peyton List, Ajiona Alexus, Dylan Sprayberry, Haley Ramm, Liana Liberato, Brent Rivera, and Jordan Rodrigues were all in attendance and stopped by to chat with us on the red carpet about the show.

In the video above, the cast not only told us about what we can expect from the first season, but also spilled some spooky BTS secrets that happened while filming some of the graveyard scenes!

Light As A Feather premiered on Hulu on Friday, October 12th and is available for streaming here.
